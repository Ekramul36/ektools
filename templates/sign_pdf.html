❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free?
Yes, completely free! No signup, no watermark, no hidden charges.
Is this a legally binding e-signature?
This tool visually places your signature image onto the PDF. It does not provide cryptographic digital-signature certification required for certain legal contracts.
Can I sign multiple pages with different signatures?
This tool places the same signature across your chosen page(s) in one go. To use different signatures per page, sign the file again for each page.
Is my PDF safe?
Yes. Your file is processed securely over SSL and deleted automatically after download.
