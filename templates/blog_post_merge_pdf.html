Why Merge PDF Files?

Merging PDFs is one of the most common document tasks for students, freelancers, and office workers. Common situations where you'd need to merge PDFs include:

  • Combining multiple scanned pages into one document
  • Putting together a portfolio or report from separate files

  • Joining split sections of a large document back together

Doing this manually — by printing and rescanning, or using paid software like Adobe Acrobat — is slow and often costs money. A free online tool solves this in seconds.

Is It Safe to Merge PDFs Online?

A common concern with online tools is data privacy — especially for sensitive documents like contracts or financial statements. When choosing a PDF merge tool, look for:

  • No permanent storage — files should be processed and then deleted from the server shortly after.
  • HTTPS encryption — your files should be uploaded over a secure connection.
  • No signup required — tools that don't ask for personal information reduce your data footprint.

EkZapp's PDF tools process files server-side and automatically delete uploaded files after processing, and no account creation is required — so your documents aren't tied to a stored profile.
