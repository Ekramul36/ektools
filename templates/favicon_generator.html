How to Use This Tool

Step-by-step guide +

1. Click the upload box or drag & drop your image (PNG/JPG/WEBP, square, ideally 512×512 or larger).
2. Check the preview to make sure it's the right image.
3. Click Generate Favicon Package — this creates all sizes (16×16 up to 512×512), favicon.ico, and site.webmanifest.
4. Click Download ZIP Package and extract it.
5. Upload the extracted files to your website's root or static folder.
6. Copy the provided HTML code and paste it inside the <head> section of your website's pages.

Frequently Asked Questions

What image should I upload? +

Use a square image at least 512×512 pixels, ideally with a transparent background (PNG). A simple logo or icon works best since favicons are displayed very small in browser tabs.

What files do I get in the package?

You get favicon.ico, favicon-16x16.png, favicon-32x32.png, apple-touch-icon.png (180×180), android-chrome-192x192.png, android-chrome-512x512.png, a site.webmanifest file, and ready-to-paste HTML code.

How do I add the favicon to my website?

Extract the ZIP, upload the files to your website's root or static folder, then paste the provided HTML link tags into the <head> section of your pages.
