Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we handle your data.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

EkZapp does not require any registration or personal information to use our tools. We collect minimal data to operate the service:

  • Files you upload for processing (temporarily stored, auto-deleted after processing)
  • Anonymous usage data via Google Analytics (page views, device type, country)
  • Standard server logs (IP address, browser type) for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Files

Files uploaded to EkZapp are used solely to perform the requested operation (e.g., compress, merge, convert). We do not:

  • Store your files permanently on our servers
  • Share your files with any third parties
  • Use your file contents for any other purpose

All uploaded files are automatically deleted from our servers after processing is complete.

3. Cookies & Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with EkZapp. This helps us improve the platform. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous data such as pages visited, time spent, and device type.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Data Security

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using SSL (HTTPS). We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access.

5. Third-Party Services

EkZapp uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — for anonymous usage tracking
  • Google Fonts — for typography
  • Remove.bg API — for background removal (images are sent to remove.bg for processing)
  • Render.com — for hosting and infrastructure

6. Children's Privacy

EkZapp does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of EkZapp after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
