❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this calorie calculator?
This uses the Mifflin-St Jeor formula which is widely used and considered accurate for most adults.
How many calories should I eat to lose weight?
Eating 500 calories less than your maintenance level typically leads to about 0.5 kg weight loss per week. Always consult a doctor before starting any diet.
