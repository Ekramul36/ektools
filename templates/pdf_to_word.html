❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Will the formatting be preserved after conversion?
We try our best to preserve the original layout, fonts, and tables. Complex PDFs with heavy formatting may have minor differences in the Word output.
Can I convert a scanned PDF to Word?
Scanned PDFs are images, not real text, so conversion results may not be accurate. Best results are with text-based PDFs created digitally.
Can I open the converted file in Google Docs?
Yes. Upload the downloaded .docx file to Google Drive and open it with Google Docs to edit it online.
Is this tool free?
Yes, 100% free. No signup, no credit card required.
