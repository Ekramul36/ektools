📖 How to Rotate a PDF

1

Upload Your PDF File

Click the upload area or drag and drop the PDF you want to rotate. Maximum file size is 50 MB.

2

Choose Rotation Direction

Select how you want to rotate — 90° Right (clockwise), 180° (upside down), or 90° Left (anti-clockwise).

3

Click "Rotate PDF"

All pages in your PDF will be rotated in the selected direction instantly.

4

Download the Rotated PDF

Your rotated PDF downloads automatically and is deleted from our server right after processing.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Will all pages be rotated?
Yes. This tool rotates all pages of the PDF in the direction you select. The rotation is applied to every single page.
What is the difference between 90° Right and 90° Left?
90° Right rotates pages clockwise (like turning a book to the right). 90° Left rotates pages anti-clockwise (like turning a book to the left).
Will the text and images in my PDF be affected?
No. Only the orientation of the pages changes. All content, text, and images remain exactly the same.

Yes, 100% free. No signup, no credit card required.
