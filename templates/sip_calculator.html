❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?
SIP stands for Systematic Investment Plan — a way to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds every month.
Are SIP returns guaranteed?
No. SIP returns are market-linked and not guaranteed. This calculator gives an estimate based on the return rate you enter.
Is this tool free?
Yes, completely free. No signup required.
