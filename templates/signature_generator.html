❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this signature legally binding?
This tool creates a visual signature image. For legally binding e-signatures on contracts, check your local e-signature laws — this tool is best for informal documents, forms, and personal use.
Does my signature get uploaded anywhere?
No. Everything happens directly in your browser — nothing is sent to our servers, so your signature stays completely private.
What format is the download?
A transparent PNG image, so you can paste it directly onto documents, PDFs, or images without a white background.
Can I use this on my phone?
Yes, the draw pad works with touch input on mobile and tablets.
Do I need to install any software?
No, everything works directly in your browser — no downloads or installs needed.
