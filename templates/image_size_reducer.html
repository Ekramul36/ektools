❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free?
Yes, completely free! No signup, no watermark, no hidden charges.
Will I get the exact target KB?
The tool gets as close as possible to your target without going over it. For very small sizes, resolution may be reduced slightly to fit.
Does it convert PNG to JPG?
Yes. For effective compression the image is saved as JPEG. Transparent areas become white.
Is my image safe?
Yes. Your file is processed securely and deleted automatically. We never store or share your files.
What is the maximum file size?
You can upload images up to 50MB in size.
