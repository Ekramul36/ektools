📖 How to Compress an Image

1

Upload Your Image

Click the upload area or drag and drop your JPG, PNG, or WebP image. Max size: 50 MB.

2

Set the Quality Level

Use the quality slider to choose your preferred output quality. Lower quality = smaller file size. 80% is a good balance for most images.

3

Click "Compress Image"

Our tool will reduce the file size while keeping the image as clear as possible.

4

Download the Compressed Image

Your compressed image downloads automatically. The file is deleted from our server immediately after.
