📖 How to Use the GST Calculator

1

Enter the Amount

Type the original price or amount on which you want to calculate GST.

2

Select GST Rate

Choose the applicable GST rate — 5%, 12%, 18%, or 28%. You can also enter a custom rate.

3

Choose Add or Remove GST

Select "Exclusive" to add GST to a price, or "Inclusive" to extract GST from a price that already includes it.

4

See the Results

The GST amount, base price, and total price are displayed instantly.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Exclusive GST?
Exclusive means GST is added on top of the price. If item costs ₹100 with 18% GST, total = ₹118.
What is Inclusive GST?
Inclusive means the price already includes GST. Use this to find out how much GST is hidden inside a price.
