📖 How to Delete Pages from a PDF

1

Upload Your PDF File

Click the upload area or drag and drop the PDF from which you want to remove pages. Max size: 50 MB.

2

Enter the Pages to Delete

Type the page numbers you want to remove. Use commas for multiple pages (2,4,6) or a dash for a range (3-5). The remaining pages will be kept.

3

Click "Delete Pages"

Our tool will remove the selected pages and create a new PDF with the remaining pages instantly.

4

Download the Updated PDF

Your new PDF without the deleted pages downloads automatically. Your original file is never modified.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I delete multiple pages at once?
Yes. Enter multiple page numbers separated by commas like "1,3,5" or a range like "2-6" to delete several pages in one go.
What is the difference between Delete Pages and Split PDF?
Delete Pages removes pages you don't want and keeps the rest. Split PDF extracts only the pages you want and discards the rest.
Will my original PDF be changed?
No. A brand new PDF is created with the remaining pages. Your original file is never modified or deleted.
Is this tool free?
Yes, 100% free. No signup, no credit card required.
