❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I calculate days between any two dates?
Yes. You can calculate the difference between any two dates — past, present, or future.
How do I find a date 90 days from today?
Use the "Add/Subtract Days" section. Set today as the start date and enter 90 in the days field.
Is this tool free?
