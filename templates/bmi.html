❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a healthy BMI?
A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered normal and healthy for most adults.
Is BMI accurate for everyone?
BMI is a general indicator. It may not be fully accurate for athletes, elderly, or pregnant women. Always consult a doctor for medical advice.
