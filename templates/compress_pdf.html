Why Compress a PDF File

Large PDFs are slow to email, get rejected by strict upload portals, and eat up storage. Compressing brings the size down while keeping the document fully readable.

📝 Exam & job forms Many portals reject uploads over 100KB–1MB.
📧 Email limits Avoid bounced emails from oversized attachments.
📶 Faster sharing Smaller files upload quickly on slow connections.
💾 Storage saving Free up space on cloud drives and devices.

A smaller file means faster uploads, quicker email sends, and no more rejected forms because of a size limit.

❓ FAQ

How much will my PDF be compressed?
It depends on the content. PDFs with many images can be reduced by 50–80%. Text-only PDFs compress less but still see a reduction.
Will compression affect the quality of my PDF?
The tool optimizes the PDF while keeping quality as high as possible. Text remains sharp and readable after compression.
Is my file safe?
Yes. All files are SSL encrypted during upload and permanently deleted from our servers after processing.

Can I compress a PDF to under 100KB or 1MB?
