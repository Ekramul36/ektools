❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much will my PDF be compressed?
It depends on the content. PDFs with many images can be reduced by 50–80%. Text-only PDFs compress less but still see a reduction.
Will compression affect the quality of my PDF?
Our tool optimizes the PDF while keeping the quality as high as possible. Text remains sharp and readable after compression.

Yes. All files are SSL encrypted during upload and permanently deleted from our servers after processing. We never store or share your files.
