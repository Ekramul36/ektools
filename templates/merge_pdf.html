📖 How to Merge PDF Files

1

Upload Multiple PDF Files

Click the upload area and select 2 or more PDF files you want to combine. You can also drag and drop them directly.

2

Check the File Order

The files will be merged in the order you selected them. The list of selected files is shown below the upload area.

3

Click "Merge PDF Files"

Our server will instantly combine all your PDF files into one single document.

4

Download the Merged PDF

Your merged PDF will download automatically. All uploaded files are permanently deleted from our server right after processing.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How many PDF files can I merge at once?
You can merge as many PDF files as you need in one go. Each individual file can be up to 50 MB.
Will the quality of my PDFs be affected after merging?
No. The original quality of all pages is fully preserved. Merging does not compress or alter the content of your PDFs.
Can I merge password-protected PDFs?
Password-protected PDFs need to be unlocked first. Use our Unlock PDF tool to remove the password, then merge.
Is this tool free?
Yes, 100% free. No signup, no credit card, no hidden charges.
