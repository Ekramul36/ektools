❓ FAQ

How many PDF files can I merge at once?
You can merge as many PDF files as you need in one go. Each individual file can be up to 50 MB.
Will merging reduce the quality of my PDF?
No. The original quality of all pages is fully preserved. Merging does not compress or alter the content of your PDFs.
Can I merge password-protected PDFs?
Password-protected PDFs need to be unlocked first. Use the Unlock PDF tool to remove the password, then merge.
Is this tool free?
Yes, 100% free. No signup, no credit card, no hidden charges.
Are my uploaded files safe and private?
Yes. Files are processed securely and automatically deleted from the server shortly after your merged PDF is generated.
Can I change the order of pages before merging?
Yes, you can drag and drop your uploaded files to arrange them in the exact order you want before merging.
