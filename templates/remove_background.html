Why Remove a Background from an Image?

Removing the background from a photo instantly makes the subject stand out, whether that's a product for an online store, a person for a passport-style or profile photo, or an object for a design project. EkZapp's Remove Background tool uses AI-based image segmentation to detect the subject automatically and produce a clean, transparent PNG — no manual selection or editing software required.

The result can be placed on any color or background, used directly with a transparent backdrop, or combined with our Passport Photo and Signature Generator tools for a complete document workflow.

Common Uses for Background Removal

  • E-commerce product photos: Clean, background-free images for online store listings.
  • ID & passport photos: Prepare a plain background before adding a required solid color.
  • Profile pictures: Create a clean headshot for resumes, LinkedIn, or social media.
  • Design & presentations: Cut out subjects for use in posters, slides, or graphics.
  • Compositing: Combine the subject with a different background image or scene.
