❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much tip should I leave in India?
In India, 5-10% is common. In restaurants with good service, 10-15% is appreciated.
Can I split the bill unevenly?
This tool splits the bill equally. For uneven splits, use the per-person amount as a base and adjust manually.
Is this tool free?
Yes, 100% free. No signup required.
