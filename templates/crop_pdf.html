📖 How to Use — Step by Step Guide

▼
  • 1
    Upload your PDF Click the upload area or drag & drop the PDF file you want to crop.
  • 2
    Set the margins to remove Enter how many millimeters to trim from the top, bottom, left, and right of every page.
  • 3
    Click "Crop PDF" The margins are removed from every page at once.
  • 4
    Download your cropped PDF Your cropped file downloads automatically, ready to use.

💡 Tips

  • Start with small values like 5-10mm and check the result before cropping more
  • The crop applies equally to every page in the PDF
  • If unsure of exact margins, a scanned A4 page usually has 10-20mm of white border
  • Your file is automatically deleted from our server after processing

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free?
Yes, completely free! No signup, no watermark, no hidden charges.
Does cropping reduce quality?
No, cropping only changes the visible page boundary — the content quality stays exactly the same.
Can I crop different margins per page?
This tool applies the same margins to every page. For page-specific cropping, crop and re-merge sections separately.
What if I enter too large a margin?
If the crop would leave less than 10 points of content, that page is left uncropped to avoid losing your content.
