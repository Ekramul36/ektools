How it works

What does this tool do?

It takes your image, optionally stamps a repeating watermark across it, then converts it into a PDF locked with the password you choose. Only someone with the password can open it.

Is my image uploaded anywhere permanently?

No. Your file is processed and the generated PDF is automatically deleted from our server after 24 hours.

What if I forget the password?

We don't store your password, so we can't recover it. Keep a copy somewhere safe before sharing the file.

Can I use this for exam forms, ID proofs, or documents?

Yes — it's a great way to share sensitive scanned documents or photos without them being freely copied or reused.
