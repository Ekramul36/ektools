❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What does EMI stand for?
EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment — the fixed monthly amount you pay to repay a loan over a set period.
Does this work for home loans, car loans, and personal loans?
Yes. Enter the correct loan amount, interest rate, and tenure for any type of loan.
Is this tool free?
