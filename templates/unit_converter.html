📖 How to Use the Unit Converter

1

Select Conversion Category

Click the category tab — Length, Weight, Temperature, Speed, Area, or Volume.

2

Enter the Value

Type the number you want to convert in the value field.

3

Select From Unit

Choose the unit you are converting from using the dropdown.

4

Click "Convert"

All equivalent values in every unit will be shown instantly.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What types of units can I convert?
Length (km, miles, feet), Weight (kg, lbs, grams), Temperature (°C, °F, K), Speed, Area, and Volume.
How do I convert Celsius to Fahrenheit?
Click "Temp" tab, enter the Celsius value, select Celsius from the From dropdown, then click Convert.
