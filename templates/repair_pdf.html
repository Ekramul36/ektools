❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free?
Yes, completely free! No signup, no watermark, no hidden charges.
Can it repair any damaged PDF?
It fixes most structural corruption issues. Severely damaged files or those with missing data may not be fully recoverable.
Will I lose any content?
The tool attempts to recover as much of the original content as possible. Portions that are unreadable due to corruption cannot be restored.
Is my PDF safe?
Yes. Your file is processed securely over SSL and deleted automatically after download.
